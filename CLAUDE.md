# Recognition Rites — Project Context

## What this is

Recognition Rites is a transformational embodiment platform offering a 12-week initiatory course, community spaces, and 1-on-1 coaching. Created by Gustavo (Gus) and Bige.

**Tagline:** Expand your edges. Keep your spark.

**Core promise:** A shift from contraction to aliveness, from self-pressure to sacred play. Life is not a problem to solve — it is a sacred game to participate in.

**Live site:** https://recognitionrites.com

## Brand Identity

### Voice & Tone
- Calm depth, warm intelligence, subtle humor, embodied steadiness, playful sovereignty
- Never preachy, never dramatic, never heavy for the sake of depth
- "Depth without gravity" — there is always a hint of a smile beneath the words
- Sentences are clear, rhythmic, breathable

### Preferred language
Recognition, Aliveness, Play, Orchestration, Harmony, Sacred, Game, Spark, Edge, Bliss, Coherence

### Language to avoid
Fixing, Broken, Toxic, Enlightenment hype, High-vibration clichés, Clinical coldness

### Colors
- Primary: Deep Teal `#0A7E8C`
- Secondary: Warm Gold `#D4A574`
- Neutral: Soft Cream `#F5F1E8`
- Accent: Muted Coral `#C97B63`
- Dark text: `#2C2C2C`

### Typography
- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Body: DM Sans (sans-serif)

### Visual style
Minimal, elegant, organic, natural textures, subtle hand-drawn elements, soft edges. No corporate feel. No spiritual cliché visuals. No harsh digital sharpness.

## Course Architecture

12 weeks, 5 phases following Shiva's fivefold acts (hidden architecture, not named to participants):

- **Phase 1: Awakening** (Weeks 1–3) —  Return to body, inner orchestra, removing lenses
- **Phase 2: Stabilizing** (Weeks 4–6)  — Nervous system, desire as navigation, ease
- **Phase 3: Dissolving** (Weeks 7–9) — Death of old self, shadow work, fear
- **Phase 4: Integrating** (Weeks 10–11) — Relational alchemy, inner authority
- **Phase 5: Embodiment** (Week 12) — Anugraha — Life as playground



Ideal customer: 30–55, high-performing professional, digitally savvy, research-oriented, burned out despite success, open to holistic practices if grounded and credible.


## Conventions

- Inline styles are fine for now — no build tools, no frameworks
- When editing copy, match the brand voice: warm, spacious, intelligent, never salesy
- Test changes visually before deploying — the cream background, teal accents, and gold dividers should remain consistent
- Videos are placeholder divs until uploaded — replace with iframe embeds when ready
- The Tally feedback form URL is: https://tally.so/r/b5OPk7

## What NOT to do

- Don't use aggressive marketing language (urgency, scarcity, FOMO)
- Don't use the word "Tantra" in headlines or CTAs
- Don't add heavy JavaScript frameworks — keep it vanilla
- Don't change the color palette without discussion
- Don't add cookie banners, pop-ups, or modal overlays — the brand is about reducing pressure, not adding it
